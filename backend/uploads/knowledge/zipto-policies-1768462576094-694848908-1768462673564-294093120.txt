ZIPTO CUSTOMER POLICIES & FAQ
==============================

REFUND POLICY
-------------
At Zipto, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, here's our refund policy:

1. Full Refund: You can request a full refund within 24 hours of delivery if:
   - The product is damaged or defective
   - You received the wrong item
   - The product quality doesn't match the description

2. Partial Refund: A partial refund may be issued if:
   - Some items in your order are missing
   - The product is slightly different from what was shown

3. No Refund: Refunds cannot be processed for:
   - Perishable items that have been opened
   - Items marked as "non-returnable"
   - Orders older than 7 days

To request a refund, contact our customer support at support@zipto.com or call 1800-ZIPTO-HELP.

DELIVERY POLICY
---------------
Zipto promises delivery in 10 minutes for most locations. Here's what you need to know:

1. Delivery Time: We aim to deliver within 10 minutes of order confirmation
2. Delivery Hours: 7 AM to 11 PM, 7 days a week
3. Delivery Fee: Free delivery on orders above ₹99. ₹25 delivery fee for smaller orders
4. Delivery Areas: We currently serve select areas in major cities

If your delivery is delayed beyond 30 minutes, you may be eligible for a discount on your next order.

PAYMENT OPTIONS
---------------
We accept the following payment methods:
- Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay)
- UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
- Net Banking
- Zipto Wallet
- Cash on Delivery (for orders under ₹500)

CANCELLATION POLICY
-------------------
You can cancel your order within 2 minutes of placing it for a full refund. After the order is being prepared:
- 50% refund if cancelled before dispatch
- No refund after the delivery partner has picked up the order

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
--------------------------

Q: How do I track my order?
A: You can track your order in real-time through the Zipto app. Go to "My Orders" and tap on your current order to see live tracking.

Q: What if my order is wrong?
A: Contact our support immediately through the app or call 1800-ZIPTO-HELP. We'll either replace the item or issue a refund.

Q: Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?
A: You can change the address within 1 minute of placing the order. After that, the order cannot be redirected.

Q: Do you deliver during rain or bad weather?
A: We try our best to deliver in all weather conditions. However, during severe weather, deliveries may be delayed for safety reasons.

Q: How do I apply a promo code?
A: Enter your promo code in the "Apply Coupon" section on the checkout page before making payment.
